Recipe #1-Vanilla Mug Cakes

  • Time:7 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
All-Purpose Flour, sifted 1 cup
Granulated Sugar 1 cup
Vanilla Extract 1 teaspoon
Baking Powder 1 teaspoon
Egg 1
Whole Milk 3/4 cup
Vegetable Oil 3/4 cup
Toppings, like Whipped Cream, Powdered Sugar or Chocolate Sauce

Step 1: Combine all ingredients and mix until just combined — do not over mix

Step 2:Divide between four mugs

Step 3:One at a time, microwave on high for 2 1/2-3 minutes, until cake rises above the mug

Step 4:Let cool for 2 minutes and garnish with desired toppings

Source for Vanilla Mug Cakes: https://www.pgeveryday.com/home/cooking-recipes/microwave-mug-cakes-3-ways

Recipe #2-Cookies and Cream Bars

  • Time:10 minutes
  • Serves:6
Ingredients Quantity
White Chocolate Chips 2 cups
Shortening or Coconut Oil 1 tablespoon
Crème filled Chocolate Cookies, coarsely chopped 20

Step 1: Line a cookie sheet with waxed paper

Step 2:In a microwave-safe bowl, heat white chips and shortening or coconut oil for 30 seconds and stir. Continue to heat for 30-second increments and stirring until melted

Step 3:Once chocolate is melted, add 2/3 of the cookie crumbs and combine

Step 4:Spread the white chocolate/cookie mixture onto the lined cookie sheet and spread as thinly as possible

Step 5:Sprinkle the remaining cookie crumbs onto the white chocolate bark

Step 6:Allow to set for 30 minutes in the refrigerator or 2 hours on the counter

Step 7:Cut into bars or break into pieces for serving

Source for Cookies and Cream Bars: https://www.pgeveryday.com/home/cooking-recipes/homemade-recipes-from-the-candy-aisle

Recipe #3-Yogurt Parfaits

  • Time:20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Mascarpone Cheese 1 cup
Non-Fat Plain Greek Yogurt 1 cup
Honey 3 tablespoons
Berries, mashed 1/2 cup
Vanilla Extract 2 teaspoons
Chilled Fresh Berries (Blueberries, Raspberries or Strawberries) 1/2 cup
Granola 1 cup

Step 1:In a medium mixing bowl, combine mascarpone, yogurt, honey and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth

Step 2:Evenly distribute mixture into the bottom of 4 glass bowls, jars or ramekins

Step 3:Layer mashed fruits, whole fruits and granola in between layers of yogurt mixture

Step 4:Top with remaining fruits and granola

Step 5:Serve immediately or keep refrigerated for up to a week as a quick snack or lunch you can grab on the way out the door

Source for Yogurt Parfaits: https://www.pgeveryday.com/home/cooking-recipes/how-to-make-yogurt-parfaits
